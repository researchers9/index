system unit assembly

steps in assembling system unit

Step 1 – Put back the motherboard
Step 2 – Put back the powersupply on its place
Step 3 – Install the Memory
Step 4 – Install the storage drives
Step 5 – Install CPU fan
Step 6 – Connect the cables
Step 7 – Put back the side cover

system unit disassembly

steps in disassembling system unit

Step 1 – Unplug your computer and peripheral items
Step 2 – Remove side covers
Step 3 – Disconnect connectors/cables
Step 4 – Remove standalone/CPU fans
Step 5 – Remove the storage drive (eg. HDD &SSD)
Step 6 – Remove memory (RAM)
Step 7 – Remove power supply unit
Step 8 – Remove the motherboard
