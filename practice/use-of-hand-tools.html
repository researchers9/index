use of hand tools

basic hand tools used in system unit assembly and disassembly

1. A tool with a flattened, cross-shaped, or star-shaped tip that fits into the head of a screw to turn it

types of screw drivers

  • Flat head screwdriver - It has a flat-bladed tip used to turn, fasten or loosen screws and bolts.

  • Phillips screwdriver - it has a head with pointed edges in the shape of a cross, which fit neatly into the cross slots of a Phillips screw.

  • Torx screwdriver - Torx screws are star-shaped head typically used in vehicles, motorcycles, bicycles, computer systems, hard disk drives and consumer electronics

  • Hex screwdriver - are great for turning small screws on sensitive electronic components. Drivers have handles with a rotating cap that allows the user to hold the shaft of the screwdriver steady while only rotating the cap at the back

    • cable tie

    2. A fastener consisting of a thin, flexible nylon strap with a notched surface, one end of which is threaded through a locking mechanism at the other. It can be used to manage the cables in system unit properly.

    anti-static wrist strap

    3. Anti static wrist straps are designed to safely ground operators working with ESD sensitive components, preventing a build-up of static electricity.

    anti-static mat

    4. It is a covering that shields an individual or a piece of equipment from static electricity discharge. These mats come in a variety of sizes and are usually placed on the floor or a workstation surface

    lan tester

    5. A LAN cable tester is used to verify that all connections are properly set up and that there aren't other connections that are unintended in the cable that is being tested. It is also use to test if a LAN cable made is working.

    crimping tool

    6. A crimping tool is a multipurpose tool use to cut and remove coats of wires, to join materials or components by pressing them together and creating a seal or crimp. One of the most common uses of crimping tools is the attachment of connectors to the end of electrical cables

    multi meter

    7. A multimeter is a measuring instrument that can measure multiple electrical properties.
